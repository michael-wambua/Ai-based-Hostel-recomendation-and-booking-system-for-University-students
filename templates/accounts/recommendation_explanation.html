How Our AI Recommendations Work

Our Smart Matching System

Our AI recommendation system analyzes your preferences and finds the best matches among available hostels. Here's how we calculate your personalized recommendations:

Budget Match (30%)

We look at your minimum and maximum budget and find hostels with room prices that fit within your range.

Location Match (25%)

We prioritize hostels in your preferred locations to minimize commute time and ensure you're in your desired area.

Room Type Match (20%)

Whether you prefer a single, double, or shared room, we find hostels offering your preferred room type.

Amenities Match (25%)

We check how many of your preferred amenities (like WiFi, laundry, security) are available at each hostel.

Match Score Colors
85%+ Excellent match - Highly recommended
70-84% Good match - Worth considering
Below 70% Fair match - May not meet all your criteria
