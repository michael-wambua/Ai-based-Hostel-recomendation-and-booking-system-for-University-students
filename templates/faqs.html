Frequently Asked Questions

Log in to your student account, navigate to the "Find Hostel" section, set your preferences, review AI recommendations, select your preferred hostel, and confirm your booking. An invoice will be automatically generated which must be paid within 3 days to secure your reservation.

Go to your student dashboard, find the booking in your "My Bookings" list, and click on the "Cancel" or "Modify" button. Please note that cancellations may be subject to the hostel's cancellation policy. If you haven't paid the invoice yet, you can simply let it expire after 3 days.

For check-in, please bring your valid student ID, a government-issued photo ID, proof of payment (receipt), and your booking confirmation. International students should also bring their passport and visa documentation. Some hostels may require additional documentation, which will be specified in your booking confirmation.

Log in to your student account, go to "Profile Settings" or "My Preferences," make the necessary changes to your information, and click "Save" or "Update." Your updated preferences will be used by our AI system to provide more accurate hostel recommendations for your future searches.

Our system allows hostels to be booked up to 6 months in advance for the upcoming academic sessions. We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for popular hostels or during peak admission periods, to ensure you get your preferred accommodation.

To access your booking history, log in to your student dashboard and navigate to the "My Bookings" section. Here you can view past bookings, current reservations, payment status, and invoices. You can also track the status of your booking including pending payment, confirmed, or cancelled.

For immediate concerns during your stay, please contact the hostel management directly using the contact information provided in your booking confirmation. Here you can set preferences such as budget range, desired location, room type (single, double, shared), amenities (WiFi, laundry, meal plans), noise level, proximity to campus, and more. Our AI system will use these preferences to generate personalized hostel recommendations for you.

For payment-related inquiries, please contact our billing department through the "Support" section.
